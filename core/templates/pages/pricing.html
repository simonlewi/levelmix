Frequently asked questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

What audio formats do you support?

Free accounts support MP3. Premium and Professional support MP3, WAV, and FLAC.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on file size and current queue load. Premium and Professional users get priority processing for faster results.

Is there a file size limit?

The free plan support files up to 300MB (approximately 3 hours of audio). This covers most DJ mixes, podcasts, and audio content. Paid plans supports up to 5GB.

Do you store my audio files?

Files are temporarily stored for processing and download. They are automatically deleted after 1 month for security and privacy.

Which platforms does LevelMix support?

LevelMix has presets for streaming (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube), podcasting, radio and broadcast, and club/festival systems. Pick your destination and we handle the technical side.
