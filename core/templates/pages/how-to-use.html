How to use LevelMix

Get the most out of your audio normalization with these tips and tricks.

Getting started

1

Upload your audio

Drag and drop your audio file onto the upload area, or click to browse your files. We support MP3, WAV, FLAC, and more formats depending on your plan.

2

Select your preset

Choose from our handcrafted presets: Streaming (-14 LUFS), Podcasts (-16 LUFS), DJ Mix (-5 LUFS), Radio (-23 LUFS), or set a custom LUFS target if you're on a paid plan.

3

Process & download

Click the process button and watch the real-time progress. When complete, download your normalized audio file. Files are kept for 30 days for your convenience.

How to get the best results

For DJs and mixtape creators

To get the best results out of LevelMix as a DJ, make sure it has a good starting point. Even though LevelMix is powerful enough to even out the most drastic volume changes, the end result will be far greater if you already try to volume match while mixing. In this situation, LevelMix will take your mix that final 10%, glue it all together and put it at your target loudness level. This will make the listening experience far more enjoyable for your audience.

Additional tips:

  • Avoid heavily compressed or clipped source audio for optimal results
  • Use higher quality source files (WAV or FLAC) when possible for best audio fidelity
  • Choose the appropriate LUFS target for your intended platform or use case

For mobile users

Uploading from Android

To upload audio from an Android device, click on Photos & Videos when the menu pops up. From here you can navigate to audio files from your local storage and choose a file to upload.

iOS Users: Simply tap the upload area and select your audio file from Files or your music library.

Understanding LUFS targets

Streaming platforms (-14 LUFS)

Perfect for Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, and other streaming services. This is the industry standard for online content.

Podcasts (-16 LUFS)

Ideal for spoken word content. Provides consistent listening experience across podcast platforms.

Club/Festival (-7 to -5 LUFS)

For DJ mixes and live performance content. Louder targets that maintain impact in high-energy environments.

Radio broadcast (-23 LUFS)

Meets broadcast standards for radio stations. Ensures compliance with regulatory requirements.

Common questions

How long does processing take?

Processing time varies based on file size and current queue load. A typical 1-hour mix usually processes in 1-3 minutes. Premium and Professional users get priority processing for faster results.

Will normalization affect audio quality?

Our normalization process uses industry-standard algorithms that preserve audio quality. We apply proper limiting and true-peak limiting to prevent clipping while maintaining the dynamic characteristics of your audio.

Can I process the same file multiple times?

Yes! You can experiment with different LUFS targets to find what works best for your content. Each processing session counts toward your monthly allowance.

What happens to my files after processing?

Your processed files remain available for download for 30 days. After that, they're automatically deleted for security and privacy. We recommend downloading your files promptly.
