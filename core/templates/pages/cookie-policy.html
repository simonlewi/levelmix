Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and allowing our website to function properly.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • To ensure our website functions correctly
  • To remember your login status and preferences
  • To analyze website usage and improve our service
  • To provide personalized features

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

ALWAYS ACTIVE

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They cannot be disabled as they are essential for providing our service.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_token Authentication and login session 7 days
user_id User identification 7 days
access_granted Beta access control 24 hours

3.2 Analytics Cookies

OPTIONAL

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting anonymous usage statistics. This helps us improve our service.

Service Purpose Duration
Google Analytics Website usage analytics (anonymized) Up to 2 years
Performance Monitoring Page load times and error tracking 30 days

3.3 Functional Cookies

OPTIONAL

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences for a better user experience.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
User Preferences Remember LUFS settings and upload preferences 1 year
UI Settings Theme preferences and dashboard layout 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze website usage (only with your consent)
  • AWS CloudFront: Content delivery and caching for better performance

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

5.2 Cookie Settings

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

5.3 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website and prevent you from using certain features.

6. Cookie Consent Record

We keep a record of your cookie preferences to comply with data protection laws. This record includes:

  • Your consent choices (essential, analytics, functional)
  • Timestamp of when consent was given
  • Version of the cookie policy at the time of consent
  • Your IP address (for verification purposes)

7. Data Processing for Analytics

When you consent to analytics cookies, we process data to:

  • Understand how our website is used
  • Identify popular features and content
  • Detect and fix technical issues
  • Improve our service based on user behavior

All analytics data is processed in an anonymized form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

8. Cookie Lifespan

Cookie Type Lifespan Description
Session Until browser closes Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
Persistent Set expiration date Remain on your device until they expire or are deleted

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw your consent for non-essential cookies at any time
  • Access information about the cookies we use
  • Request deletion of your cookie consent data
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Show the cookie banner again to request renewed consent
  • Notify you via email if you have an account with us
