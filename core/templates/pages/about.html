Technical excellence

Professional measurement

Our analysis engine uses ITU-R BS.1770-4 standards for accurate loudness measurement, providing reliable and consistent results across all your content.

Smart processing

Our adaptive algorithms apply the right processing approach for each content type—aggressive leveling for music and streaming, or natural dynamics preservation for spoken word.

Platform optimization

Each preset is carefully tuned for its specific use case, whether it's punchy DJ mixes, consistent streaming content, natural podcasts, or broadcast-ready material.

Quality assurance

Every processed file is automatically checked to ensure clean output with proper headroom, no clipping, and high-quality encoding preserved.
